CHAPATHI

Chapathi is made up of wheat flour.It is a good for health.Most of the people ate chapathi,because of dieting.It is good for dieting.Chapati, also known as roti, rotli, safati, shabaati, phulka, chapo, and roshi, is an unleavened flatbread originating from the Indian subcontinent and staple in India, Nepal, Bangladesh, Pakistan, Sri Lanka, East Africa, Arabian Peninsula and the Caribbean.
