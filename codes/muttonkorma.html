MUTTUN KORMA

Traditional mutton korma or gosht korma is a rich Lucknowi stew of lamb or goat meat slow cooked in brown onions, nuts, spices, yogurt and aromatic essences like saffron and kewda (screw pine). The aura of this korma is unique, rich, smooth, delicate and exceptional.Though often dismissed as a fatty meat, in flavor, not much can compete with a good mutton curry.
