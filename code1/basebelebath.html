BESE BELE BATH

At some stage, though, bisi bele bath escaped the palace walls and found its way to the famed vegetarian eateries of Udupi in western Karnataka. From there, a vegetable-laden version spread throughout the state and bisi bele bath turned into a hearty farmer's meal, consumed mid-morning after a stint in the fields.With low carbohydrate content, it is categorised under low glycemic index food and is ideal for diabetic patients as it helps in reducing the blood sugar levels. It is gluten free and is a good source of iron. Savour health with Organic Roots infused with drooling flavours of Bisi Bele Bath.
