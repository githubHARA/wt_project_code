KASHMIR PULAW

Kashmiri Pulao is a very easy veg pulao which is aromatic and mildy sweet, flavoured with spices and saffron with the crunch of fruits, dry fruits and fried onions. The variation of making kashmiri pulao uses a different mix of fruits and dry fruits but in general for fruits : apple,pomegranate and pineapple is used.t not only improves the taste of your food, it also has a lot of health benefits to offer.
